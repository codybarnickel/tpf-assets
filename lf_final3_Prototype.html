Trans-Pecos Strategic
Place Identity Framework

A governing framework for Trans-Pecos communities and the region they share. Each Local Frame gives an individual community its own documented character, values, and development standards, built through assessment and held by the community. The Regional Frame is where those positions unite, giving civic leaders across the Trans-Pecos a shared foundation and a collective voice in the conversations that shape what comes next.

This prototype includes one fully populated example frame (Sandpoint, Idaho). All other frames carry functional content describing what assessment produces at the regional, county, and city level.
